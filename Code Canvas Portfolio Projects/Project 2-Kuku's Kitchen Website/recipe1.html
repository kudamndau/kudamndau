Caprese Salad Skewers
Ingredients

  • Cherry tomatoes (1 pint)
  • Fresh mozzarella balls (1 cup)
  • Fresh basil leaves (1 bunch)
  • Balsamic glaze
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Wooden skewers

Method

  1. Preparation:
    • Wash the cherry tomatoes and basil leaves thoroughly.
    • Drain the fresh mozzarella balls if they are stored in brine.
    • Soak wooden skewers in water for at least 30 minutes to prevent them from splintering.
  2. Assembly:
    • Take a wooden skewer and thread on a cherry tomato, followed by a fresh basil leaf, and then a mozzarella ball.
    • Repeat the process until each skewer is filled with a delightful assortment of tomatoes, basil, and mozzarella.
  3. Arrangement:
    • Arrange the assembled skewers on a serving platter or dish.
  4. Finishing Touches:
    • Drizzle balsamic glaze and extra virgin olive oil over the skewers.
    • Sprinkle a pinch of salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Presentation:
    • Serve the Caprese Salad Skewers on the platter, allowing the vibrant colors and enticing aroma to captivate your guests

Tips

  • Use fresh and high-quality ingredients for the best flavors.
  • Experiment with different types of cherry tomatoes and mozzarella for variety.
  • You can prepare these skewers in advance and refrigerate them until ready to serve.

This delightful Caprese Salad Skewers recipe is not only visually appealing but also a burst of fresh and tangy flavors. Enjoy these as a light and elegant appetizer at any gathering!
