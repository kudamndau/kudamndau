Chocolate Lava Cake
Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 4 ounces high-quality dark chocolate
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • Pinch of salt
  • Optional: Vanilla ice cream and fresh berries for serving

Method

  1. Preheat Oven
    • Preheat your oven to 425°F (220°C). Grease and flour four ramekins.
  2. Melt Chocolate and Butter
    • In a heatproof bowl, melt the butter and dark chocolate together. You can do this using a double boiler or by microwaving in short 20-second bursts, stirring between each burst until melted and smooth.
  3. Mix Sugar and Eggs
    • In a separate bowl, whisk together the powdered sugar, eggs, egg yolks, and vanilla extract until well combined.
  4. Combine Chocolate and Egg Mixtures
    • Gradually add the melted chocolate mixture to the egg mixture, whisking continuously until smooth.
  5. Add Flour and Salt
    • Sift in the all-purpose flour and a pinch of salt. Gently fold the dry ingredients into the chocolate mixture until just combined. Be careful not to overmix.
  6. Fill Ramekins
    • Divide the batter evenly among the prepared ramekins.
  7. Bake
    • Place the ramekins on a baking sheet and bake in the preheated oven for about 12-14 minutes. The edges should be set, but the center should still be soft.
  8. Serve
    • Allow the lava cakes to cool for a minute. Run a knife around the edges and invert each cake onto a plate. The center should be gooey and molten.
  9. Optional
    • Serve with a scoop of vanilla ice cream and fresh berries for an extra indulgence.

Tips

  • The key to a gooey lava center is not overbaking. The outer edges should be firm, but the center should remain soft.
  • You can prepare the batter in advance and refrigerate it. Just make sure to bring it to room temperature before baking.

Indulge in the rich and decadent experience of Chocolate Lava Cake, a classic dessert that never fails to impress with its gooey, molten center. Enjoy the delightful combination of warm chocolate and a velvety texture!
