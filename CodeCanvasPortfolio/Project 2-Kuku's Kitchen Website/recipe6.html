Grilled Salmon with Lemon-Dill Sauce
Ingredients

  • 4 salmon fillets
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons fresh dill, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Zest of one lemon
  • Juice of half a lemon
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon honey
  • Lemon wedges for serving

Method

  1. Prepare Salmon:
    • Pat the salmon fillets dry with paper towels. Season both sides with salt and black pepper.
  2. Preheat Grill
    • Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  3. Make Marinade
    • In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, chopped dill, minced garlic, lemon zest, lemon juice, Dijon mustard, and honey to create the marinade.
  4. Marinate Salmon
    • Brush the salmon fillets with the marinade, ensuring they are well coated. Let them marinate for at least 15-20 minutes to absorb the flavors.
  5. Grill Salmon
    • Place the salmon fillets on the preheated grill. Grill for about 4-5 minutes per side or until the salmon is cooked through and has beautiful grill marks.
  6. Serve
    • Transfer the grilled salmon to a serving platter. Drizzle any remaining marinade over the top. Garnish with additional fresh dill and lemon wedges.

Tips

  • If you don't have a grill, you can also cook the salmon in a grill pan on the stovetop or bake it in the oven.
  • Adjust the grilling time based on the thickness of your salmon fillets.
  • The lemon-dill sauce adds a refreshing and zesty flavor to the grilled salmon.

This Grilled Salmon with Lemon-Dill Sauce is a delightful dish that brings out the natural flavors of the salmon while adding a burst of citrusy freshness. Enjoy this elegant and healthy meal!
