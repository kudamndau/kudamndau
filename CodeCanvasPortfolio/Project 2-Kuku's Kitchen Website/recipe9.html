Tiramisu
Ingredients

    For the Coffee Soak
  • 1 1/2 cups strong brewed espresso or very strong coffee, cooled
  • 2 tablespoons coffee liqueur (optional)
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
    • For the Mascarpone Filling
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 1/2 cups mascarpone cheese, softened
  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
    • For Assembling
  • 24-30 ladyfinger cookies
  • Unsweetened cocoa powder, for dusting
  • Chocolate shavings, for garnish (optional)

Method

    Prepare the Coffee Soak
  1. Brew Coffee
    • Brew a strong cup of espresso or coffee. Allow it to cool to room temperature.
  2. Add Sugar and Liqueur
    • In a bowl, combine the cooled coffee, granulated sugar, and coffee liqueur (if using). Stir until the sugar is dissolved. Set aside.
    3. Prepare the Mascarpone Filling
  3. Whisk Egg Yolks
    • In a heatproof bowl, whisk together the egg yolks and granulated sugar. Place the bowl over a pot of simmering water (double boiler) and whisk constantly until the mixture thickens and becomes pale in color.
  4. Add Mascarpone
    • Remove the bowl from heat and whisk in the mascarpone cheese until smooth. Allow it to cool.
  5. Whip Cream
    • In a separate bowl, whip the heavy whipping cream and vanilla extract until stiff peaks form.
  6. Combine Mixtures
    • Gently fold the whipped cream into the mascarpone mixture until well combined. Be careful not to deflate the whipped cream.
    7. Assemble the Tiramisu
  7. Dip Ladyfingers
    • Dip each ladyfinger into the coffee mixture, ensuring they are soaked but not overly saturated.
  8. Layer the Bottom
    • Arrange a layer of soaked ladyfingers in the bottom of a serving dish or individual glasses.
  9. Add Mascarpone Mixture:
    • Spread half of the mascarpone mixture over the soaked ladyfingers.
  10. Repeat Layers
    • Repeat the layers with another layer of soaked ladyfingers and the remaining mascarpone mixture.
  11. Dust with Cocoa
    • Dust the top with a generous layer of unsweetened cocoa powder.
  12. Chill
    • Cover the dish or glasses with plastic wrap and refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight to allow the flavors to meld.
  13. Garnish and Serve
    • Before serving, garnish with chocolate shavings if desired.

Tips

  • Ensure the coffee mixture is cool before dipping the ladyfingers to prevent them from becoming too soggy.
  • Tiramisu develops better flavor when allowed to chill in the refrigerator for a longer time.

Enjoy the indulgent layers of Tiramisu, a classic Italian dessert that combines the bold flavors of coffee, creamy mascarpone, and delicate ladyfingers! Buon appetito!
